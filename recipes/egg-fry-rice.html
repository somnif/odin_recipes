Egg Fried Rice:

  • 3 cups cooked rice, chilled
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish
  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble until cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil to the skillet. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the mixed vegetables and cook until heated through.
  4. Add the chilled rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the rice is heated through.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and stir to combine.
  6. Add the cooked eggs back to the skillet and stir to distribute evenly.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
