Lasagne:

  • Lasagne sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg until well combined.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce. Top with a layer of lasagne sheets, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the sheets.
  6. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of lasagne sheets on top. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the lasagne.
  7. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagne is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
